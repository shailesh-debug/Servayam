Animation Portfolio Summary

The Servayam portfolio includes interior animation, exterior visualization, 3D modeling showcases, and original channel-led content development. We design projects to support brand campaigns, product communication, and architectural storytelling where visual clarity matters.

Interior Animation

Interior project work includes Modern Loft, Luxury Residence, Minimalist Studio, and Industrial Space. These projects focus on spatial narrative, camera choreography, materials, and lighting that communicate design intent to buyers, investors, and stakeholders.

Exterior Visualization

Exterior project work includes Urban Facade, Landscape Flow, Architectural Marvel, and Scenic Route. This category is designed for architecture and real-estate storytelling where scale, context, and environmental realism are required.

3D Modeling

3D model showcases include Character Design A, Character Design B, Environment Model, and Prop Collection. We develop production-ready assets for cinematic campaigns, digital experiences, and animation pipelines with topology and rendering constraints in mind.

Services

Servayam services include 3D animation, interior visualization, exterior visualization, film production support, and video editing. Projects can be delivered as short-form social content, ad-ready video assets, launch films, or long-form storytelling packages depending on business goals.

Our workflow starts with alignment around audience and objective, then moves through concept planning, style direction, animation execution, and review cycles. We support founders and marketing teams who need both quality craft and dependable turnaround.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project turnaround?

Project timelines vary by complexity, but many 3D product videos and short animations are delivered in two to three weeks. Larger narrative or environment-heavy projects can run four to six weeks with milestone-based review checkpoints.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Servayam is based in India and supports global clients through structured async communication and scheduled review windows.

How many revisions are included?

Revision rounds are staged through concept, production, and final polish phases so decisions are made at the right point in the pipeline.

Which formats do you deliver?

We deliver MP4, MOV, ProRes, transparent background outputs, and platform-specific versions such as 9:16, 16:9, and 1:1.

Do you handle scripting and creative direction?

Yes. We can support projects from a raw brief to final production with scripting, storyboard direction, style framing, and post-production coordination.

Original Content

Servayam Originals includes Giggle Filmz and long-form educational storytelling formats where we test narrative hooks, pacing, and attention design in live publishing environments. This experience improves our client production work because we understand what audiences actually watch.
